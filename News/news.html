Jasprit Bumrah to miss Sri Lanka ODIs

Jasprit Bumrah will not play the three-match ODI series against Sri Lanka starting Tuesday. The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) sources have said a call has been taken not to rush him into action so soon. He is not among the players who have reached Guwahati, the venue for the first ODI.

If I was bowling to Surya, I'd be disheartened: Pandya

Hardik Pandya always seems to have the right words. A lot of his teammates playing under him will swear by that, but so will everyone who tuned in to watch the Rajkot T20I and were left short of words. After all, how else can you describe these unbelievable knocks from Suryakumar Yadav? His latest one, a whirlwind 45-ball hundred, featured even sharper paddles and deeper scoops, played off balls even further outside off and higher up his torso. But the India captain, when asked about the innings, had the perfect set of words in his inimitable style.

"If I was bowling to him, I'd be disheartened ... because of the kind of shots he has been playing, " Pandya told host broadcaster Star at the post-match presentation ceremony. "He's been surprising everyone every evening and he's letting everyone know that you know what, this batting is very easy, you know. He's been actually playing blinders after blinders and, which is now becoming a habit and, which is fantastic for him.

WIPL media rights on January 16 with conditions attached

The media rights auction of the Women's Indian Premier League (IPL) has been postponed by four days and it will now be conducted on January 16 but the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has fixed the date with a few conditions attached. It says by any chance the WIPL, scheduled to be launched in the first week of March, does not take place this year, there will be rebidding next year.

If for any reason, 5 (five) Teams are not constituted to play in the League in Season 2023 or the League is not organized in Season 2023, then all the Media Rights Agreements shall stand terminated, " the BCCI has mentioned in the Invitation To Tender (ITT) document.
